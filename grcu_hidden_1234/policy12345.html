Entering Orders Using Order Audit Trail System and Order Tracking System

Order Audit Trail System ("OATS") – FINRA requires that all registered members who receive and/or handle any orders in NMS stocks and OTC equity securities comply with OATS rules.  Rule 600(b)(47) of Regulation NMS defines an NMS stock as "any security or class of securities for which transaction reports are collected, processed, and made available pursuant to an effective transaction reporting plan, or an effective national market system plan for reporting transactions in listed options.”

In accordance with FINRA Rules 7410-7470, the firm is required to electronically capture and report to FINRA OATS specific data related to receiving, handling, and execution of orders.

As required by FINRA, OATS reportable event times are captured by the firm's order entry and trading systems, which are synchronized to the National Institute of Standards & Technology ("NIST") atomic clock.
